Getting Started with UDLex — A Quick Start Guide

Published 2025-09-01 | Written by UDLex Team

Welcome to UDLex — a community and resource hub for Roblox developers. This guide walks you through joining our community, configuring your profile, sharing projects, finding collaborators, and making the most of UDLex's features.

Why join UDLex?

UDLex brings together developers, educators, and creators who build on Roblox. Joining helps you:

  • Showcase projects and get feedback from experienced builders.
  • Discover collaborators and contributors for game projects.
  • Access tutorials, tooling, and curated resources.
  • Participate in community events like jams and workshops.

1. Join the Discord

The quickest way to get started is to join our Discord at discord.gg/unifydevs. When you join we suggest you:

  1. Introduce yourself in the community's introductions area and briefly describe your interests or projects.
  2. Familiarize yourself with the community guidelines and any verification steps for new members.
  3. Explore the project showcase, collaboration spaces, and support channels to find relevant conversations and opportunities.

2. Set up your profile and portfolio

Make your first impression count:

  • Add a clear avatar and banner to your Discord and project pages.
  • Prepare a short project blurb and include screenshots or a short demo.
  • Include links to repositories or forum posts so others can review your work.

3. Share your project

When sharing, include the essentials so people can quickly understand and test your work:

  1. A concise project overview (what it is and what problem it solves).
  2. Installation or play instructions and required Roblox versions.
  3. Screenshots or a short demo video/GIF.
  4. Links to repositories, models, or deployment previews.

4. Find collaborators

Use the community's collaboration spaces to post roles you need (scripter, builder, artist). Be specific about skills, expected time commitment, and how to contact you.

Tips for getting useful feedback

  • Ask targeted questions (e.g., "how can I optimize NPC pathfinding?").
  • Provide code snippets and clear reproduction steps.
  • Be open to suggestions and follow up with results.

Helpful resources

  • Tutorials: See our articles section for step-by-step guides.
  • Tools: Explore shared tooling and integrations available in the community resources.
  • Events: Watch for jam announcements and workshop schedules.
