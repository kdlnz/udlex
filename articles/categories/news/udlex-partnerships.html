How to Partner with UnifyDevs

Published 2025-08-20 | Written by UDLex Team

UDLex runs a curated partnership program for developer communities, toolmakers, and organizations that align with our mission of supporting Roblox creators and builders. Partnerships provide selected communities with visibility on UDLex, promotional support, and access to joint initiatives. This article explains what we offer, who is eligible, how to apply, and what partners can expect.

What UDLex Provides to Partners

  • Site listing: A dedicated partner entry (logo, short description, and link) on udlex.com/partners.
  • Promotional exposure: Monthly highlights, social posts, and newsletter features when appropriate.
  • Event support: Co-hosted developer jams, workshops, and showcase events promoted across our channels.
  • Shared resources: Access to common documentation, tooling, and optional bot integrations maintained by UDLex.
  • Integration assistance: Guidance for integrating our post/listing system or APIs into partner platforms.

Who Is Eligible

We look for communities and organizations that meet these baseline criteria:

  1. Aligned mission: A focus on development, learning, collaboration, or tools for Roblox creators.
  2. Active community: A sustained community presence (typically 300+ members) with regular engagement.
  3. Professional conduct: Responsible moderation and policies that follow Discord and platform terms.
  4. Responsive staff: A leadership or staff team that can coordinate events and promotions.

Expectations & Terms

Partners agree to:

  • Provide an official logo and a short description for the UDLex listing.
  • Coordinate on announcements when applicable and credit UDLex for shared promotions.
  • Maintain community standards that align with our values and platform policies.

Integration & Promotion Examples

Examples of collaborative activities:

  • Co-hosted workshops (tutorial nights, tool demos).
  • Shared resource pages and cross-posted announcements.
  • Partner spotlight articles and monthly features.

FAQs

Is the partnership paid?
Basic listings and many partnership features are offered at no cost. For larger integrations or sponsored placements, we may discuss paid options with commercial partners.
How long does a partnership last?
Partnerships are ongoing but reviewed periodically. We ask partners to remain active and to communicate changes to their communities.
What if we don't meet the member threshold?
Smaller communities are welcome to apply. We consider engagement quality, event frequency, and alignment with our goals.
